  # Ergonomic Design Approach

This document describes the ergonomic principles behind SmartBite’s physical design.

---

## 1. User Comfort

- Mouthpiece shape  
- Material selection  
- Bite pressure distribution  
- Long‑term comfort  

---

## 2. Safety

- Non‑toxic materials  
- Smooth edges  
- Moisture resistance  
- Sensor isolation  

---

## 3. Fit & Form

- Universal fit vs. size variants  
- Thickness constraints  
- Placement of electronics  

---

## 4. Hygiene

- Cleaning  
- Replaceable covers  
- Moisture sealing
